Internet Browser Start Guide

Surf the web on your TV using the Wii U GamePad controller. Share with everyone in the room—or browse privately on the GamePad.

Read on for features that only the Wii U console can bring to your Internet experience.

View websites and play web videos on the TV screen for everyone to enjoy—in HD on supported TV sets.

Please note, as of the 10/01/2013 system update, you will also be able to view PDF files.

Using only the Wii U GamePad, you can browse the Internet with ease while someone else uses the TV.

Scroll through pages, select links, and more, all using the Wii U GamePad touch screen or buttons.
You can also use the Wii Remote or Wii U Pro Controller.

Zoom in or out with the Right Stick or the buttons on the GamePad.

Hold the ZL and ZR Buttons and tilt the GamePad to scroll up and down a web page.

You can also scroll with the buttons or stylus.

While a web video is playing on the TV, use the GamePad to find another video.

Hold and release the X Button to open the curtains with a drumroll.

Upload screenshots of the game you're playing to the Internet.

Use Wii U Image Share to easily post your screenshots to Twitter, Facebook, or Tumblr.

♦ You must install the 10/01/2013 system update to use this feature. Please note that you may not be able to take screenshots in some games or during certain scenes.

The web is never farther away than the HOME Button. Easily look up hints and tips while your game is suspended.
